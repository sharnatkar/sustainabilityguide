About Us

We're three young professionals looking for ways to live a more sustainable lifestyle. We believe you can improve the choices you make to help take care of the environment without compromising your lifestyle. We've picked areas such as food and fashion to look at because these are areas where sustainable choices are becoming more and more apparent. This website helps create a directory of brands and restaurants you can visit to shop and eat without feeling guilty about the environmental impact.
